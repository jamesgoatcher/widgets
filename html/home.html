JPG.Widgets
The Widgets suite is a collection of small scale programs similar to those found on codepen.io.
They are written in modular, class exported modern JS.
Some solve common production needs while others just showcase a development aspect I appreciate.
Fundamentally, this is a collection of rogue studies that needed a home.

Final Fantasy VI
A contender for the best RPG of all time, I wanted to recreate a slice of the game. I chose to focus on the battle sequence. Here, you can select your party of 4 from 8 characters. You will be assigned a random location and random enemy to defeat. Each character and enemy comes equipped with unique stats and abilities. Best of luck!
Status: Incomplete
FEATURES:
  • CT is uniquely calculated and grows
  • 100% CT triggers action menu and stops other CT
  • Fight ability
  • Damage applies
  • Victory sequence and battle music
  • Special abilities and Items as valid actions
